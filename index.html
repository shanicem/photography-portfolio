Hello,

My name is Hershey Rendon!

I have to say I'm not fond of writing things like these but I love hearing other people's stories. I think that's one of the things that really drew me to photography. I get to see your story through my lens and then retell it in a beautiful photo.

Since the birth of my son I've truly realized how fleeting time is. He will never be this little again. Which brings me to the other reason I love photography: I get to freeze time. For those few photos, time is frozen and we get to keep a little more of those moments.

My purpose for my work is to help you capture the right now, to help you suspend time and to give you beautiful memories.

Thank you so much for visiting and I look forward to meeting you!
